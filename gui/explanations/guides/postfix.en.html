Postfix (cond + ortho)

postfix_ortho_cond — Caption-conditional postfix with structural orthogonality. A small cond_mlp reads the pooled caption embedding and emits (S(c), λ(c)) per caption — K(K-1)/2 + 1 scalars — instead of a direct K·D postfix tensor. Each caption's postfix is Cayley(S(c) − S(c)ᵀ) @ basis · λ(c), so postfix(c) @ postfix(c).T = λ(c)² · I_K structurally: uniform per-slot magnitude within a caption, magnitude varies across captions.

Basis is the top-K right singular vectors of cached _anima_te.safetensors, row-shuffled deterministically by ortho_basis_seed. Trainable surface ~390k params at K=32 / hidden=256 / D=1024 (vs ~8.65M for a legacy unconstrained cond postfix at K=64). See docs/proposal/orthogonal_postfix.md §C.

Preprocessing

Three artifact lanes feed training:

1. Text caching — tokenizes captions through Qwen3 + T5 and writes {stem}_anima_te.safetensors sidecars. When shuffle variants > 0, each cache holds N captions and the dataloader samples one per training step (turn on use_shuffled_caption_variants in your method config to use them).

2. SAM3 masking — finds text-bubble regions using natural-language prompts. Saved to masks/sam/.

3. MIT masking — runs a manga-specific text segmenter trained on glyph-level data. Saved to masks/mit/.

The Merge step unions SAM + MIT into masks/merged/. When the merged dir exists, training subsets pick it up automatically (see masked_loss in the method config).
